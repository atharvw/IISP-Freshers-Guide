Vision

To give overview of various aspects of IBDP program like CAS, NGO’s, Summer school, internships etc to Fresher’s ( students and parents who are new to IBDP program) and help them to plan the first year and utilise their time optimally.

Mission

The awareness of different aspects of IB program will help the students to plan their timeline to complete these criterions and last minute rush can be avoided. This will in turn help them to concentrate on their academics as many activities can be done during various school vacations.
