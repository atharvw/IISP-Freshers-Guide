Tree Public


Tree Public is a registered environment NGO methodically working towards a-forestation by way of macro scale Tree Plantation in Pune.

Our core focus is to work towards reducing the Carbon Footprints by way of planting native Indian trees like Neem, Peepal, Banyan (Vad), Umbar, Kashid, Tamarind etc. known for their properties of purifying the atmosphere and creating clean breathing air.
