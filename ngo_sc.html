Help Age India


HelpAge envisions a society where elderly have the Right to an active, healthy and dignified life. The mission is to work for the cause and care of disadvantaged older persons and to improve their quality of life.

The aim is to help elders rebuild their own lives and take charge of their own future, restoring within them a sense of self worth and confidence.
