Summer Schools - USA

Stanford University

Join a global community of students at one of the world’s leading institutions of research and learning.
Our Summer Session students take on challenging academics for college credit while balancing educational
and social activities. Discover a passion to become a scholar or find your way to change the world.
Whatever path you choose, you’ll have a learning experience that makes a true and lasting impact.

450 Serra Mall, Stanford, CA 94305, USA

Application online:
https://summercollege.stanford.edu/admission/tuition

+1 650 723 2300




Harvard University

Imagine studying at Harvard for seven weeks, fully immersed in the college experience. Whether you
choose to live on campus, commute, or take courses online, you’ll have the opportunity to explore
complex topics, gain valuable life skills, and form lasting bonds.Not only will participation in
the Secondary School Program enhance your college application, but the experiences you gain will
also make you a more informed applicant.

Cambridge, MA 02138, USA

Application Online:
http://www.summer.harvard.edu/programs/ssp/default.jsp

+1 617 495 1000




UCLA

Summer Sessions is a unique opportunity to advance your academic career. During the summer, many
academic courses that make up UCLA's regular curriculum are offered in two sessions, Session A and
Session C . With over 100 courses to choose from, including online courses, you can have an
unforgettable summer no matter where you are.

Los Angeles, CA 90095, USA

Application online:
http://www.summer.ucla.edu/highschool/overview.htm

+1 310 825 4321




Massachusetts Institute of Technology (M.I.T.),

MIT does not offer a traditional open-enrollment summer school program where any high school
student can come to campus to take courses and live in the dorms. However, several partner
organizations run small, specialized programs on campus. If you'd rather study the human genome
or build a robot than memorize this year's summer TV reruns this is a program for you.

77 Massachusetts Ave, Cambridge, MA 02139, USA

Application online:
http://mitadmissions.org/apply/prepare/summer

+1 617 253 1000
