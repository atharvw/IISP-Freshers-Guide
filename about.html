About Us


We joined Indus International School, Pune in August 2016 for IBDP program. Diploma program is very demanding and develops one overall. At Indus there are various clubs like website designing, cookery, art and club, MUN club, music etc. We enrolled ourselves to the Website designing program in the first semester. We were introduced to HTML and Java at our school in 8 th , 9 th and 10 th . But to make a good professional website , we first decided to learn further. We took a crash course in HTML5, CSS, JQuery, Bootstrap and started building the website. That gave us inspiration to develop this “FRESHERS GUIDE “ website for the school. Our class of IBDP has 145 students , all from various schooling background. For our CAS, we were to work in NGOs, internships, do personal projects, and so on. While interacting with other classmates we realized that all the problems that we faced while finding information about NGOs, organizations which can give us internships, how many hours we were expected to work , how to present our NGO work documents , what exactly counted as activities in CAS and so on... Our parents were trying to help us find all the information and still weren't quite successful. So we decided to work some solution for this problem. During our Christmas break the idea of making this website struck us. We thought that the new students to Indus will benefit from all the information gathered by us and the Placements office. So we complied all of the information we could find and encoded it in our website. We thought that the website was the good platform as the students and parents could access it easily. We spent hours together and came up with this. We really hope you find it helpful and enjoy your experience through the IBDP!
