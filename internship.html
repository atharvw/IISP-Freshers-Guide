Pratham Education

At the Pratham Open School Programme, we endeavor to provide an opportunity for children to engage with the process of learning. It is an attempt to provide space for children to test and explore the world through their learnings. Digital content of videos, stories and games is provided to children in 400 villages across 3 states. The idea is to develop application-based knowledge inputs which are accessed by children in the 8-13 years age group. We would love to get inputs from people from all walks of life and different age groups to develop the content as well as various applications of the content.
